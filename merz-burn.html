Earth Burn at the Merz Barn


24mins, 2011

A film about Cumbrian rural charcoal making practices on the site of the last great Dadaist artist Kurt Schwitters's final major work.

With thanks to Cylinders Estate and the Merz Barn, Elterwater, Cumbria, and the Slade School of Fine Art's student organised summer school.


Screened at Open City Docs, London, 2013, and various other festivals

WR SAUNDERS 2012
