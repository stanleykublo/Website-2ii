One Day as I was Driving Home


15mins, 2021

A podcast guest tries to tell the presenter a story, but they seem not to understand. He says it changed his life, and yet it seems impossible, beyond a certain point, to get the interviewer to grasp what he's saying.

Focuses on the limits of verbal communication and connection, and the risks involved in attempting to convince people of a truth you hold dear. Also about what would happen if one day driving home you just stopped the car and waited, for a very long time.

Starring: Johnnie Gill and Tom Blake
