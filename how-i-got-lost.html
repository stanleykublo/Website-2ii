How I Got Lost


27mins, 2014

A man looks for meaning in his life and in the world around him. Instead of finding it, he gets lost on a search that includes mushroom foraging on golf courses, musical instruments wedged in piles of rubbish, and silent snowed-in housing estates due for demolition.

A meditation on the nature of self, and the search for meaning and identity in a world that can appear, and be, fragmented, choatic, and overwhelming.


Exhibited at de Ateliers, Amsterdam, 2014, and ASC St. George's Circus, London, 2014
